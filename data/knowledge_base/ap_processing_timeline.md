# AP Payment Timeline by Vendor Tier

Category: billing

## Processing Timeline Overview

The Accounts Payable (AP) department processes vendor payments on a fixed schedule. Processing time depends on the vendor's tier and the invoice amount.

## Standard Processing Times

| Vendor Tier | Invoice Amount | Expected Processing Time |
|-------------|---------------|--------------------------|
| Platinum    | Any amount    | 5-7 business days        |
| Gold        | Under $10,000 | 7-10 business days       |
| Gold        | Over $10,000  | 10-15 business days      |
| Silver      | Under $10,000 | 10-15 business days      |
| Silver      | Over $10,000  | 15-20 business days      |
| Standard    | Under $10,000 | 15-20 business days      |
| Standard    | Over $10,000  | 20-30 business days      |

## Payment Run Schedule

- **Tuesday**: Wire transfers and ACH payments for approved invoices
- **Thursday**: ACH payments and check runs
- **Cut-off**: Invoices must be approved by 3:00 PM EST the day before a payment run

## Expedited Processing

Vendors can request expedited processing in these cases:
- Critical supply chain dependency (requires manager approval)
- Contractual early payment discount (e.g., 2/10 Net 30)
- Overdue invoices past 30 days

To request expedited processing:
1. Submit request via VQMS Portal > Expedited Payment Request
2. Include invoice number and reason for expedition
3. Processing time reduced to 3-5 business days for approved requests

## Important Notes

- All processing times are counted from the date of successful three-way match
- Public holidays and weekends are excluded from business day counts
- Partial payments may be issued if a discrepancy exists on part of the invoice
- Remittance advice is emailed to the vendor's registered email within 24 hours of payment
