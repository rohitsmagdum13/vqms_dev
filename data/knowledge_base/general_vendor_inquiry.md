# General Vendor Inquiry Guide

Category: general

## Vendor Onboarding

New vendors are onboarded through a standard process:
1. Submit vendor registration form via the VQMS Portal
2. Procurement team reviews within 5 business days
3. Compliance check (KYC/AML) takes 3-5 business days
4. Vendor master data created in Salesforce CRM
5. Welcome email sent with portal credentials and payment setup instructions

## Portal Access

### First-Time Login
- URL: https://vqms.company.com/portal
- Use credentials from the welcome email
- Must set a new password on first login
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) is required

### Password Reset
- Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
- Reset link sent to registered email
- Link expires after 24 hours
- Contact vendor-support@company.com if email not received

### Account Locked
- Account locks after 5 failed login attempts
- Auto-unlock after 30 minutes
- Contact support for immediate unlock if urgent

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I update my banking information?**
A: Log into the portal > Settings > Payment Information. Changes require 2FA verification and take effect after 3 business days for security review.

**Q: How do I add a new contact for our vendor account?**
A: The primary account contact can add new users via Portal > Settings > Team Management. New users receive an invitation email.

**Q: What file formats are accepted for invoice submission?**
A: PDF, XML (UBL 2.1), and CSV. Maximum file size: 10 MB per attachment.

**Q: How long are records retained?**
A: All records (invoices, POs, payments, correspondence) are retained for 7 years per compliance requirements.
